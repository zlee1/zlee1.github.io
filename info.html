Wordle-N Info

This game is a lot like the original Wordle game.
The only real difference here is that words can be sizes different than 5 and the number of guesses can be different than 6.
Wordle-N allows for words ranging from 2 to 22 characters long!


How to Play

This Wordle variation has fairly straightforward rules and they are as follows:

  • Enter a word by either typing or pressing buttons on the built-in keyboard,
  • Ensure that your guess is a legal word (legal words came from extended scrabble list),
  • Submit a guess, press the Enter key or the Guess button on the built-in keyboard,
  • When a guess is submitted, you will receive information on your guess,
    • A green cell means the letter is correct and in the correct position,
    • A yellow cell means the letter is in the word but in the wrong position,
    • A gray cell means the letter is not in the word.
Green Yellow Gray
  • To win, you must use the provided information to guess the word.
That's all there is to it. Go play the game!
